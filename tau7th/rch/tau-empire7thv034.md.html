    ## Systems ### Support
    Advanced targeting system
    Confers Precision Shots.
    Blacksun Filter
    Confers Night Vision and immunity from [Blind](#blind).
    Drone controller
    All MV1, MV7 and MV71 in the same unit use the bearer's BS instead of his own. If a unit contains more of the same, use the highest BS.
    DS8 tactical support turret
    If the unit remains stationary in its Movement Phase, you can set up the tactical support turret on an area of open ground, within 2" of a model from its unit. and more than 2" away from any enemy models. Once setup the turret cannot move.
    BS 3. It can fire normally as any other model. It can fire on the turn it is set up.
    Enemy models cannot attack or affect the turret in any way, but it is immediately removed as a casualty if there are no other models within 2" of it, or if an enemy model approaches within 2" of it.
    Once removed it can be returned to play in a future Movement phase as described above.
    EMP
    Field Amplifier Relay
    Confers 5+ shield.
    Ghostkeel Electrowarfare suite

    Vector Strike
    When Swooping or [Zooming](#zooming).
    At the end of the Movement Phase, nominate one enemy unit not locked in combat that the model has moved over that turn. That unit takes 1 hit.

    These hits:
    • Have the Ignores Cover special rule.
    • Do not benefit from any of the model's special rules
    • Are resolved against the target's side armour.
    A model that used this rule counts as having already fired one weapon in the following Shooting phase. However the weapons it fires can choose a different target.
