    ## Special
    Acute Senses
    At least one model with the rule.
    Can re-roll to see which table edge they arrive from.
    Armourbane
    Confers an additional D6 for armour penetration.
    Blind
    At least one model with the rule.
    Any unit hit by a weapon with this special rule must take an Initiative test at the end of the current phase. If the test is failed, all models in the unit are reduced to WS and BS 1 until the end of their next turn.
    Bulky
    Models count as two for the purposes of Transport Capacity
    Deepstrike
    All models in the unit must have the special rule
    Roll for the arrival of all the units and then deploy one model first, anywhere on the table, roll for scatter to determine the model's final position, next arrange the unit's remaining models around the first one in base contact with the first forming a circle around it.
    They treat all difficult terrain as dangerous terrain.
    The units can fire as normal as having moved in the previous Movement Phase. Vehicles count as having moved at [Combat Speed](#combatspe).
    Deep Strike Mishap Table
    D6 - Effect
    1: Terrible Accident!
    The entire unit is destroyed!
    2-3: Misplaced
    Your opponent may deploy the unit anywhere on the table, following the rules, but without rolling for scatter.
    4-6: Delayed
    The unit is placed in Ongoing Reserves
    Fear
    At the start of each Fight sub-phase a unit in base contact with one or more enemy models with the rule must take a Leadership test before any blows are struck. If failed all models have WS reduced to 1 until the end of the sub-phase.
    Feel no Pain
    Can't be taken against Destroyer or Instant Death.
    After an unsaved wound a unit can roll again on a 5+.
    Independent Character
    They can join other units or other Independent Characters but they cannot join units that contain vehicles or Monstrous Creatures.
    • Joining and leaving a unit
      • During the movement phase they have to be within 2" unit coherency distance from a friendly unit.
      While he is part of a unit, he counts as part of the unit for all rules purposes, thoug he still follows the rules for [characters](#character)
    • Look Out, Sir
      • They pass the rolls on a 2+
    • Heroic Morale
      • If a unit contains one or more Independent Characters it tests as if it had at least 25% remaining.
    • Infiltrate
      • An Independent Character without the Infiltrate special rule cannot join a unit of infiltrators during deployment
    Infiltrate
    At least one model with the rule.
    They are deployed at last, after all other units have been deployed. If both sides have infiltrators, the higher D6 will decide and they alternate deploying these units.
    They can be set up anywhere on the table that is more than 12" from any enemy unit, as long as no deployed enemy unit can draw line of sight to them. This includes buildings. Alternatively, they can be set up anywhere on the table more than 18" from any enemy unit.
    • Outflank
      • At least one model with this special rule.
      When this unit arrives from Reserves, but not Ongoing Reserve, the controlling player rolls a D6:
      1-2: The unit comes from the table edge to the left of their controlling player's own table edge
      3-4: The unit comes from right
      5-6: THe player can choose
      The models move as described for other Reserves
    • Infiltrate and Scout
      • If a unit has both Infiltrate and Scout, they can deploy twice following both rules.
    Instant Death
    If a model suffer an unsaved Wound from an attack with this special rule, it is removed as a casualty.
    Melta
    Confers an additional D6 for armour penetration at half range or less.
    Move Through Cover
    At least one model with the rule.
    Confers an extra D6 when rolling through difficult terrain and it is not slowed by charging through difficult terrain. Furthermore they automatically pass Dangerous Terrain tests.
    Night Vision
    At least one model with the rule.
    Ignores the effects of Night Fighting
    Precision Shots
    A Wound obtained from a 6 To-Hit is allocated against a model of your choice.
    Look Out, Sir roll allowed.
