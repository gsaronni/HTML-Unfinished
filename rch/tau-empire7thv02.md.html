### A Ghost Who Walks Among Us

The Warlord, and his unit, move 3D6" when making Jet Pack thrust moves.

### Bonding Knife Ritual

Confers Heroic Morale to a unit IF all the models have this rule

### Defender of the Greater Good

All Stealth Shas'ui and Shas'vre models in the same unit as have Sworn Protector special rule.

### Failure is not an option

All models within 12" of the Ethereal use his Leadership

### Fire Team

IF a unit includes 3 vehicles or Monstruous Creatures it Confers +1 Ballistic Skill

### Invocation of the Elements

At the start of each of the Ethereal's Movement phases, choose one of the powers to give to an unit within 6": - *Calm of Tides*: Subtract 1 from any Morale tests made for affected units. - *Storm of Fire*: : You may re-roll hit rolls of 1 in the Shooting phase for affected units that remain stationary in the Movement phase. - Sense of Stone: Whenever an affected unit suffers an unsaved wound, roll a D6. On a 6, that wound is ignored. - *Zephyr's Grace*: You can re-roll the dice for affected units when they Advance.

### Supporting Fire

When an enemy declares a charge, all friendly with this special rule within 6" can choose to fire Overwatch
